# Dental Services

> ⚠️ DEMO DATA — confirm the real service list with the clinic before going live.

## General Dentistry

- Dental check-up and consultation
- Scaling and polishing (teeth cleaning)
- Tooth-colored fillings
- Tooth extraction
- Root canal treatment
- Dental crowns and bridges
- Dentures (complete and partial)

## Cosmetic Dentistry

- Teeth whitening
- Veneers
- Smile makeover consultation

## Children Dentistry

- Children's dental check-ups
- Fluoride application
- Fissure sealants
- Habit counseling (thumb sucking, bottle feeding)

## Orthodontics

- Braces consultation and referral
- Clear aligner assessment

## Emergency Dental Care

- Severe toothache relief (examination first)
- Broken or chipped tooth care
- Knocked-out tooth first-aid guidance and urgent appointment
- Dental abscess assessment and referral

## What We Do Not Do Online

The chatbot cannot diagnose conditions, prescribe medicines, or suggest dosages. Every
treatment starts with an in-clinic examination by a qualified dentist.
