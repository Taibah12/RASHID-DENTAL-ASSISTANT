# Frequently Asked Questions

> ⚠️ DEMO DATA — review each answer with the clinic before going live.

## Is the chatbot a real dentist?

No. This is an AI assistant that answers general questions about the clinic using verified
information files. It cannot examine you, diagnose conditions, or prescribe medicines.
Always consult a qualified dentist for clinical advice.

## Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Appointments are recommended so you do not have to wait. Walk-in patients are seen when a
slot is free, and emergency cases are prioritized.

## How long does a check-up take?

A standard consultation usually takes 20 to 30 minutes.

## Is teeth cleaning painful?

Scaling and polishing is generally comfortable. If you have sensitive teeth or gums, tell
the dentist before the procedure starts.

## Do you treat children?

Yes. We see children for check-ups, fluoride application, and sealants. A parent or
guardian must accompany patients under 18.

## Can I get a cost estimate before treatment?

Yes. After examination, the clinic shares a written estimate before any treatment begins.

## What if I have a dental emergency at night?

Call the clinic phone number. If you have uncontrollable bleeding, a severe facial injury,
or swelling that affects breathing or swallowing, go to the nearest hospital emergency
department immediately.

## Does the clinic use sterile instruments?

Yes. Instruments are sterilized between patients following standard infection-control
protocols.

## How do I cancel or change my appointment?

Call or WhatsApp the clinic at least 4 hours before your slot, or ask the chatbot to
submit a new appointment request.
