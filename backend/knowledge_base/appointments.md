# Appointments

> ⚠️ DEMO DATA — confirm the real booking policy with the clinic before going live.

## How to Book

You can request an appointment in three ways:

1. Through this chatbot — the assistant collects your details and sends a request to the clinic
2. By phone or WhatsApp during opening hours
3. By visiting the clinic reception

## Information We Need for a Request

- Patient name
- Contact number
- Preferred appointment date
- Preferred appointment time
- Requested service or a short reason for the visit

## Request vs Confirmation

An appointment submitted through the chatbot or website is a **request only**. It is **not
confirmed** until clinic staff review it and contact you. Please wait for a confirmation
call or message before visiting.

## Same-Day and Emergency Slots

A limited number of same-day slots are kept for urgent cases. Call the clinic directly for
the fastest response.

## Cancellation and Rescheduling

- Please inform the clinic at least 4 hours before your slot if you cannot come.
- You can reschedule by phone, WhatsApp, or by asking the chatbot to submit a new request.

## Late Arrival Policy

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, your slot may be given to a waiting patient and
you may be asked to wait for the next available time.

## What to Bring

- Any previous dental records or X-rays (if available)
- A list of medicines you currently take
- Your confirmation message or call reference
